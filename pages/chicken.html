Oven Baked BBQ Chicken

OVEN BAKED BBQ CHICKEN

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces skin on, bone in chicken thighs/drumsticks (~3 lbs)
  • 3 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1.5 tsp Light Brown Sugar
  • 1.5 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 Medium Yellow Onions
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1 cup BBQ Sauce (my favorite go-to is Sweet Baby Ray's)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F
  2. Pat dry chicken with paper towel then transfer to bowl.
  3. Combine salt, brown sugar, cumin, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder in small bowl
  4. Coat entire chicken pieces with spice rub
  5. Peel and halve onions. Thinly slice length wise.
  6. Cover baking sheet with foil and transfer onion to baking sheet. Drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Place chcken skin side up on top of onions
  8. Roast on top rack for 40-45 min. Blister for 2-4 minute under broiler
  9. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  10. While chicken is resting, combine apple cider vinegar and BBQ sauce.
  11. Flip chicken upside down and paint with BBQ sauce mixture.
  12. Broil for 2-4 minutes (until blistered)
  13. Flip chicken again, paint skin side with BBQ mixture and broil for 2-4 minutes.
  14. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes before serving
